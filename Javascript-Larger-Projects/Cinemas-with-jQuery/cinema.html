About Academy Cinemas

Located in the beautiful Portland, Oregons Pearl district!

Our reels have been spinning since 1959. Showing our guests a great time is our passion and seeing their smiles is what we're all about.

Accomodations

All of our auditoriums are equipped with brand new Christie 3D® sounds systems!

Relax in our extra-wide, plush-cushioned recliners. Seating is on a first-come first-served basis. Our seating arrangement is disability friendly

Our auditoriums are climate controlled to approximately 72° year-round.
